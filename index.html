Fully experience

the European wedding of your dreams...

At the Amore Palazzo, which translates to “Love Haven”, we will provide you with the fairy tale wedding you’ve always dreamed of. Our gorgeous venue was designed in 1860 by Michangelo Guggenheims, and overlooks the Venice bridges and waterways. The banquet hall can fit up to 300 people, and packages include catering, bar services, decorations, and more.
